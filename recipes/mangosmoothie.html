Mango Banana Smoothie

Description

This mango banana smoothie is great for kids.I always look for quick, easy, healthy, and delicious recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Chop up mangos & banana
  2. Combine choped up pieces of mangos & banana with vanilla yogurt,and milk in a blender
  3. Blend until smooth
